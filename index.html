Platform Features

Verified Donors

Secure donor verification with blood group and health check reports to ensure safety and reliability. All donors undergo comprehensive medical screening and background checks before approval.

Multiple Components

Support for whole blood, plasma, white blood cells, and platelets donation with specialized matching. Advanced algorithms ensure optimal component allocation based on patient needs and donor compatibility.

Rewards & Recognition

Earn points, certificates, and social recognition for your life-saving contributions. Track your impact with detailed statistics and receive exclusive badges for milestones achieved.

NGO/Hospital Collaboration

Seamless partnership with healthcare organizations for drives and emergency response. Real-time coordination tools enable efficient campaign management and resource allocation.

Location-Based Matching

Find nearby donors instantly during emergencies with precise location-based matching. Advanced geolocation technology ensures rapid response times and optimal donor-patient proximity.

Instant Alerts

Real-time notifications for urgent requests and matching donors in critical situations. Multi-channel alert system ensures immediate communication through SMS, email, and push notifications.
